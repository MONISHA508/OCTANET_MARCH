VARIETIES

Marigold

It hold ancient significance in Indian culture. Their bright yellow color exudes cheerfulness and resilience. Perfect for summer and autumn gardens

Dahlia

Dahlias, with their elegant blooms, thrive as great summer flowers in India. Gardeners take special care to grow these beauties for flower competitions

Hibiscus

Another flower commonly used for worship, hibiscus is associated with Goddess Kali. Its empowering red color makes it a favorite. Hibiscus grows year-round in various regions of India

Jasmine

Known for its unique sweet fragrance, jasmine is widely used in cosmetics and Ayurveda. It graces weddings and social events, blooming from spring to fall

Rose

Rose flowers are a popular crop for both domestic and commercial cut flowers. There are over three hundred species rose native to Asia, used for Perfume, Food, drink and Medicine with very rich in vitamin C.

Tulip

True love, as the name also suggests, is the right meaning of a tulip flower. All colours but mostly red is the chosen one when you try to express your emotions to a loved one with a slight individuality.
